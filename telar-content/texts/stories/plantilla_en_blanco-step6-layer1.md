During Halloween weekend in 1993, students protested police barricades placed throughout Del Playa, leading to clashes and unrest. 

UCSB alumnus Geoff Green later recalled that the recent memory of the 1992 Rodney King riots and national conversations surrounding police brutality made students especially sensitive to aggressive policing tactics. 

As officers flooded Isla Vista in unprecedented numbers, many students feared not only disorder within the crowds, but also the possibility of excessive force from law enforcement itself.
