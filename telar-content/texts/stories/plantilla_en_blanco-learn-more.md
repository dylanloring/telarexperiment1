This year in 2026, to suppress the traditional hoopla of Deltopia festivities, Santa Barbara authorities implemented a 72 hour festival ordinance banning amplified music throughout the April 4th weekend. 

More than 300 officers from agencies across California enforced the restrictions, effectively shutting down the crowds that had drawn roughly 25,000 attendees the year before. 

Officials would then justify this crackdown through public safety statistics. 

Police indicate that Deltopia weekend in 2025 resulted in 130 medical calls, 485 citations, and 84 arrests, while the heavily regulated 2026 weekend produced only 7 medical calls, 42 citations, and 6 arrests.
