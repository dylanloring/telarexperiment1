Unlike previous years, many of the people attending Halloween in 1992 were not UCSB or Santa Barbara City College students, but visitors arriving from across California. 

According to the Daily Nexus, the enormous crowds contributed to dramatic increases in accidents, crimes, violations, and arrests. 

More than one thousand arrests were made over the course of the night, while reports described numerous partygoers suffering injuries and two individuals being left in critical condition. 

Despite the scale of the disorder, law enforcement officials considered the operation a success because officers were able to suppress multiple riots and regain control of the streets.
