title: "Learn more"
---

In 1997, UCSB students and law enforcement officials held pre-Halloween meetings to discuss expectations for the holiday and explore safer ways to celebrate. 

These conversations aimed to reduce conflict by clarifying police policies while also encouraging students to consider alternative events outside of the traditional Del Playa street gatherings. 

Through these discussions, students gained a clearer understanding of why heightened police presence had become standard practice in Isla Vista.
