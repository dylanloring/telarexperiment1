Although the formal Five-Year Plan had ended, Santa Barbara Sheriff's Department officials made clear that police presence in Isla Vista would remain strong.

Sergeant Mike Burridge emphasized in a Daily Nexus interview that law enforcement would continue operating under a zero-tolerance approach, citing the need to maintain a safe environment for students.

Under this policy, any violations of law would be met with immediate citations, and large-scale police deployment would continue throughout Halloween weekend.
